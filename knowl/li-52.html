Item. Type A.

If \(D=\{(x,y,z)\colon (x,y)\in \mathcal{R}, p_1(x,y)\leq z\leq p_2(x,y)\} \text{,}\) where \(\mathcal{R}\) is an elementary region of \(\R^2\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation} \iiint\limits_\mathcal{D}f(x,y,z)\, dV=\iint\limits_{\mathcal{R}}\left(\int_{p_1(x,y)}^{p_2(x,y)}f(x,y,z)\, dz\right)\, dA\text{.}\tag{1.5.4} \end{equation}
