Item 1.

First we parametrize \(\mathcal{S}\text{.}\) Observe that intersecting \(\mathcal{S}\) with the plane \(z=z_0\) yields a circle centered at \((0,0,z_0)\) of radius \(R=\cos z_0 \text{,}\) which we can parametrize as

\begin{equation*} (R\cos\theta, R\sin\theta, z_0)=(\cos z_0\cos\theta, \cos z_0\sin\theta, z_0)\text{.} \end{equation*}

Letting \(z\) vary between \(-\pi/2\) and \(\pi/2\text{,}\) we get the parametrization

\begin{align*} \boldr(\theta, z) \amp = (\cos z\cos\theta, \cos z\sin\theta, z) \\ \mathcal{R} \colon 0\amp \leq \theta\leq 2\pi, -\pi/2\leq z\leq \pi/2 \text{.} \end{align*}
