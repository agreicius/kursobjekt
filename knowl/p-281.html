Let \(D\) be the region lying within the sphere. Observe that the function in question is \(f(x,y,z)=\sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}\text{.}\) In spherical coordinates the solid sphere is described as

\begin{equation*} \{(\rho, \phi, \theta)\colon 0\leq \theta\leq 2\pi, 0\leq \phi\leq \pi, 0\leq \rho\leq R\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

We have

\begin{align*} \operatorname{vol} D \amp = \iiint\limits_D \sqrt{x^2+y^2+z^2}\, dV \\ \amp=\int_0^{2\pi}\int_0^\pi\int_0^R\rho\cdot \rho^2\sin\phi\, d\rho\, d\phi\, d\theta \amp \text{(spher. subst.)}\\ \amp = 2\pi\int_0^\pi\sin\phi\, d\phi \int_0^R\rho^3\, d\rho\\ \amp = 2\pi (-\cos\phi)\Bigr\vert_0^{\pi}\left(\frac{1}{4}\rho^4\right)\Bigr\vert_0^R\\ \amp =\pi R^4\text{.} \end{align*}
