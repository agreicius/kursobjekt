Assume \(f\) is integrable over the measurable solid region \(\mathcal{D}\subseteq \R^3\text{.}\) The average value of \(f\) over \(\mathcal{D}\text{,}\) denoted \(\operatorname{avg}_\mathcal{D}(f)\text{,}\) is defined as

\begin{equation} \operatorname{avg}_\mathcal{D}(f)=\frac{1}{\operatorname{vol} \mathcal{D}}\iiint\limits_\mathcal{D}f(x,y,z)\, dV\text{.}\tag{1.5.13} \end{equation}
