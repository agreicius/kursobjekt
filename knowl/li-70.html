Item 3. Additivity.

If \(\mathcal{D}=\mathcal{D}_1\cup \mathcal{D}_2\text{,}\) where \(\mathcal{D}_1\) and \(\mathcal{D}_2\) are non-overlapping, then

\begin{equation*} \iiint\limits_{\mathcal{D}}f(x,y,z)\, dV=\iiint\limits_{\mathcal{D}_1}f(x,y,z)\, dV+\iiint\limits_{\mathcal{D}_2}f(x,y,z)\, dV\text{.} \end{equation*}
