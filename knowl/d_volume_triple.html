Definition 1.5.5. Volume of solid region.

A bounded region \(\mathcal{D}\subseteq \R^3\) is measurable if the constant function \(f(x,y)=1\) is integrable over \(\mathcal{D}\text{.}\) In this case we define the volume of \(\mathcal{D}\) as

\begin{equation} \operatorname{vol}\mathcal{D}=\iiint\limits_\mathcal{D} 1 \, dV\text{.}\tag{1.5.3} \end{equation}
