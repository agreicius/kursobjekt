Corollary 1.5.10. Fubini's theorem for boxes.

If \(f\) is continuous on the box \(B=[a,b]\times [c,d]\times [e,f]\) then

\begin{align*} \iiint\limits_B f(x,y,z)\, dV \amp =\int_a^b\int_c^d\int_e^f f(x,y,z)\, dz\, dy\, dx\\ \amp =\int_c^d\int_a^b \int_e^ff(x,y,z)\, dz\, dx\, dy\\ \amp \hspace{10pt}\vdots \\ \amp =\int_a^b\int_e^f\int_c^d f(x,y,z)\, dy\, dz\, dx\\ \amp =\int_e^f\int_a^b \int_c^df(x,y,z)\, dy\, dx\, dz\text{.} \end{align*}
