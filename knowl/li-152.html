Item 2.

Assemble the necessary ingredients:

\begin{align*} \boldr_\theta \amp = \angvec{-\cos z\sin\theta, \cos z\cos\theta, 0}\\ \boldr_z\amp =\angvec{-\sin z\cos\theta, -\sin z\sin\theta, 1} \\ \boldr_\theta\times \boldr_z \amp=\angvec{\cos z\cos\theta,\cos z\sin\theta, \sin z\cos z\sin^2\theta+\sin z\cos z\cos^2\theta} \\ \amp =\angvec{\cos z\cos\theta,\cos z\sin\theta, \sin z\cos z}\\ \abs{\boldr_\theta\times \boldr_z} \amp = \sqrt{\cos^2(1+\sin^2 z)} \\ \amp =\abs{\cos z}\sqrt{1+\sin^2 z}\\ \amp =\cos z\sqrt{1+\sin^2z} \text{,} \end{align*}

where the last equality follows since \(\cos z\geq 0\) for \(z\in [-\pi/2, \pi/2]\text{.}\)
