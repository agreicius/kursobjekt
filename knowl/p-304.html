Assume random variables \(X\) and \(Y\) have joint probability density function

\begin{equation*} f(x,y)=\begin{cases} 6x^2y\amp (x,y)\in [0,1]\times [0,1]\\ 0\amp (x,y)\notin [0,1]\times [0,1] \end{cases}\text{.} \end{equation*}

  1. Compute \(\mu_X\) and \(\mu_Y\) for any two random variables \(X\) and \(Y\) with joint probability distribution function \(f\text{.}\)

  2. Compute the probability that \(X\geq Y\text{.}\)
