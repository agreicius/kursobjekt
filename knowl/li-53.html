Item. Type B.

If \(D=\{(x,y,z)\colon (y,z)\in \mathcal{R}, q_1(y,z)\leq x\leq q_2(y,z)\} \text{,}\) where \(\mathcal{R}\) is an elementary region of \(\R^2\text{,}\) then

\begin{equation} \iiint\limits_\mathcal{D}f(x,y,z)\, dV=\iint\limits_{\mathcal{R}}\left(\int_{q_1(y,z)}^{q_2(y,z)}f(x,y,z)\, dx\right)\, dA\text{.}\tag{1.5.5} \end{equation}
