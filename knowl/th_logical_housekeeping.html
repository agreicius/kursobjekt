Theorem 2.3.17. Logical housekeeping.

Let \(\boldF\) be a vector field on the set \(D\subseteq\R^n\text{.}\) For statements involving curl, assume the partial derivatives of the component functions of \(\boldF\) are continuous.

\begin{align*} D \text{ open}\colon \boldF=\nabla F\amp\implies \boldF \ \text{ conservative} \impliedby \nabla\times \boldF=\boldzero \end{align*}
